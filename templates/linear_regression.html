What is Linear Regression?

The main objective of utilizing linear regression models is to assess the impact of independent variables on the dependent variable and acquire estimated data through the development of a statistical mathematical mode[1]

Basic Type of Linear Regression[2]
  • Simple Linear Regression

    Linear regression is one of the most common techniques of regression analysis when there are only two variables.

  • Multiple Linear Regression

    Multiple regression is a broader class of regressions that encompasses linear and nonlinear regressions with multiple explanatory variables


Correlation of Coefficient[3]

The coefficient of determination (r2) is the proportion of the total variation in the dependent variable (Y) that is explained or accounted for by the variation in the independent variable (X).

Advertising Sales Dataset using Linear Regression Model[4]

The dataset on advertising encompasses the correlation between advertisement expenditures across various channels such as radio, TV, and newspapers and the resulting sales revenue.

The goal is to comprehend the influence of advertising budgets on the overall sales performance.

Guide for Application in Advertising Sales Prediction[4]

To utilize the advertising sales prediction application, simply click on the UI keyword in the navigation bar next to the HOME keyword to display the UI section. The UI application is designed for predicting sales revenue in advertising sales. Additionally, to utilize the application, input the advertising budgets for TV, Radio, and Newspaper, and then click the submit button to generate the sales prediction.
